Definitions

What is a computer?
A computer is a technological device used for doing calculations that humans might not be able to do sometimes. For the most part, a computer can be on for a very long time while a human can only be awake for so many hours.
What about my device?
Your desktop, laptop, mobile phone, game console, microwave, vacuum, car, etc.
These are all examples of computers.
What Operating System (OS) Do I Have?
The predominance of desktops and laptops have Windows 10 or Windows 11.
Your phone is using its own operating system, most likely based on Linux.
