The World of Technology.

OpenEyes is your "one-way ticket" to a deep dive into computers.
You can learn about any topic if you invest enough time into it. Technology has always been looked at as a difficult concept, with only some individuals out there able to understand it. This is not true, and OpenEyes wants to show you just how easy it all is! OpenEyes is a non-profit public source that contains tools, definitions and many more items that are helpful if you wish to educate yourself in the technological field. We are an organization, will the sole purpose of mastering your mindset in computers. Just within a few minutes, you can learn how computers operate, how the internet functions, how to better maintain your desktops and laptops, and much more!
